Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information, which your computer or mobile device downloads when you visit a website. When you return to the website - or visit websites that use the same cookies - they recognise these cookies and therefore your browsing device.

How do we use cookies?

Our website uses cookies to personalise the experience of users without an account, such as tracing user's preferences. For more information on how we use your data, see our Privacy Policy

How do I opt out of cookies?

To opt out of cookies, you can stop visiting the website, or prevent your browser from accepting cookies. However, these methods can limit the functionality of the website.
