Overview:

To upload your HTML5 or WebGL game, just create a game. Then, create a game part, and upload your main html5 file there, as well as additional directories storing additional files.

Creating A Game:

To create a game, go into your account settings, by clicking your profile picture in the top-right corner of the website.

Next, in the column on the left side of your screen, click the text that says "Games".

Using the provided form, fill out your game's title, summary, and choose its genre. The genre of the game determines how the website's recommendation algorithm handles it.

That's it! Now, you can create the parts of your game, where you will actually upload your files.

Creating A Game Part:

To create a game part, go back to your "Games" profile settings.

Next, click on the green button next to the game you want to create the game part in.

In the provided form, fill out your game part's title, summary, and choose from a list of genres. The genre of the game part determines how the website's recommendation algorithm handles it

Fill out the next provided form, with your game part's thumbnail, HTML file named "index.html", and a folder containing additional js and css files. It is recommended that images be uploaded in JPEG format, while your additional folder only contains files filled with text.

That's it! You can now play your web game. You can also see statistics on your web game through your account settings.
